Overview

Purpose

We love creating memories through the great sport of whitewater rafting. Through the website, now potential customers can browse different activites, see pricing, and book their rafting trip without having to call our office or book in person. It will make our business more accessible to more people. Along with accessibility, our website will provide more visibility for the business. It will appear in online searches, and we can spread the word on other platforms, linking the posts or advertisements to this website.

Audience

We cater to people looking for an adventure. Whether it be a family outing, or a weekend getaway with friends, Whitewater Rafting Company is here for our customers' thrill-seeking needs. On this website, there will be pricing information, schedules, and recommended routes to take based on skill level. One they have selected the right trip, they can easily book and pay for it on the website. Most customers access this site from their desktop or laptop computers at home, but they can also book a trip just as easily on the go from a mobile device.
