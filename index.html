Rachel Marin | WDD 130

Hi! I am from Washington state and I love to cycle and slackline. Many people do not know what slacklining is, but it is basically a ratchet strap stretched between two trees that you are supposed to balance on. Some people learn how to do tricks on it, but I am content just balancing across and not injuring myself. I also like baking, althougn it is such an unhealthy hobby that I have to limit myself a lot. I am attending Brigham Young University - Idaho right now, and I hope to figure out what I will major in soon! I was born in Washington, but then my family moved to Montana, and we lived there for about two years. Then, we moved to New York, where we lived for about 4 years. After that, we moved back to Washington, but to a town called University Place, where we have lived ever since. We liked University Place so much that we have lived in three different homes in the city, and my family now owns the house we live in.
